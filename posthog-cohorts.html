Cohorts Cheatsheet

Turn “users who did X” into a reusable handle across PostHog

Why use it

Cohorts turn “users who did X” into a reusable handle you can target with surveys, feature flags, insights, and replay filters. Stop hunting through raw user lists. Build the cohort once, then navigate by it across PostHog.

Build once, navigate by it. Chained workflows, per-segment comparisons.

Honest caveats (from the team)

No survey-answer filter yet. Use survey branching for answer-level routing.
Autocapture cohorts at scale = labor-intensive. Works for early-stage; refresh tax grows.
Cohort path is buried in some views. Replay frustration-signals filter takes ~4 clicks.

Tips & tricks

1
Pin cohorts to your sidebar. Multiple customers got lost mid-workflow. The sidebar is customizable, but almost nobody discovers this. Cheap win.
2
Cohort drill-down as navigation, not analysis. Replay path: dashboard → funnel → cohort → recording. Build the funnel, materialize the failing cohort, drill in.
3
Survey respondents → cohort → follow-up. Cohort survey takers, then target with a deeper second survey or interview booking. Surveys become a research-recruitment funnel.
4
Autocapture actions as cohort seeds. Pre-instrumentation products can still build behavioral cohorts: autocapture → action → cohort. Unblocks early-stage teams.
5
Geographic cohorts for multi-market redesigns. US vs. UK cohorts on replay frustration signals give per-market before/after for any rollout.

Customer patterns in the wild

🎓 EdTech startup (~900 users)

~50% response
PM wanted to survey only students who used the AI tutor “enough.” Built a weekly autocapture → static cohort → re-target loop. Painful but worked.
Cohorts unlock behavioral targeting even without instrumented events, if you can stomach the refresh tax.

🏢 B2B SaaS (1.5M MAU)

replaced Pendo
Growth PM migrated all surveys to PostHog: satisfaction survey → respondents become a cohort → that cohort gets an interview-booking survey.
Cohorts + surveys = automated user research recruitment, no standalone tooling.

🌍 Multi-country SaaS

phased rollout
PM built US and UK cohorts, applied them to replay frustration signals during a beta. Clean before/after view per market, used to gate the wider rollout.
Cohorts as a per-market product-quality lens during a phased rollout.
